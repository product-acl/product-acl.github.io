Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Agile Catalyst Labs ("we," "us," or "our") operates the SplitVibe mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials. If you sign in using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we receive basic profile information from those services (such as your name and email address).

Authentication Data

We use Firebase Authentication to manage user accounts. Firebase generates authentication tokens that are stored securely on your device and used to verify your identity.

Receipt and Session Data

When you use the App, we collect receipt data including photos of receipts and the items, prices, and totals extracted from them. We also collect session data such as participant names, item assignments, tip amounts, tax amounts, and currency preferences that you create as part of bill splitting sessions.

Push Notification Tokens

If you grant permission, we collect push notification tokens through Firebase Cloud Messaging to send you relevant notifications about your sessions and account.

Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect usage data such as screen views, feature usage events, session creation events, and general app interaction patterns. This data helps us understand how the App is used and improve the user experience.

Subscription Data

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat collects and processes subscription status, purchase history, and entitlement information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Process and store receipt data for bill splitting
  • Send push notifications about session activity
  • Manage your subscription and entitlements
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App
  • Respond to your requests, comments, or questions

3. How We Store Your Data

Your account and session data is stored securely using Firebase services and our backend database. Receipt images are stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

  • Firebase Authentication – for user account management and sign-in
  • Firebase Analytics – for usage analytics and app improvement
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging – for push notifications
  • RevenueCat – for subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Google Sign-In – for authentication via Google accounts
  • Apple Sign-In – for authentication via Apple accounts

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen in the App. When you delete your account, all of your personal data, session history, receipt data, and any other information associated with your account will be permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers. Receipt images stored locally on your device will also be removed.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only with the third-party service providers listed above, as necessary for the operation of the App, or if required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
