SPINUP

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Agile Catalyst Labs ("we," "us," or "our") operates the SpinUp mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App. SpinUp is designed with privacy in mind — we do not operate user accounts or transmit personal data to our own servers.

1. Information We Collect

Data stored locally on your device

SpinUp stores your game preferences (such as drinking mode default) and active session state (current card, score, deck) on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Purchase and entitlement data (RevenueCat)

When you make an in-app purchase, we use RevenueCat to process and manage your entitlement. RevenueCat collects and processes:

  • Purchase receipts and transaction history from the App Store or Google Play
  • Device identifiers used to associate purchases with your device
  • App Store or Google Play account identifiers

RevenueCat acts as a data processor on our behalf. Their privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID)

The App declares use of the advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android). On iOS, this is used by RevenueCat solely for purchase attribution if you grant App Tracking Transparency permission. You can deny this permission and the App will continue to work normally.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not create or store user accounts
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not record gameplay, card responses, or any in-game content you interact with
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or behavioral tracking
  • We do not collect location data

3. How We Use Information

The limited information processed through RevenueCat is used solely to:

  • Verify and restore your one-time purchase ("Unlock All")
  • Grant or revoke premium content entitlement

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with one third-party service:

  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management. Privacy Policy

Purchases are ultimately processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play), each with their own privacy policies. We do not handle payment card information.

5. Age Requirement

SpinUp is intended for users aged 18 and older. The App contains content related to alcohol consumption and mature themes. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor is using the App, please contact us.

6. Data Deletion

Because we hold no personal data on our servers, there is no account to delete. Local app data can be removed by uninstalling the App from your device. For RevenueCat purchase records, you may contact us and we will request deletion on your behalf.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we hold about you. Given that we hold minimal data (purchase records via RevenueCat only), requests can be directed to us at the contact below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
